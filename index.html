Introducing amberOS

amberOS, inspired by SapphireOS is a Windows modification for both Windows 10 and Windows 11 that makes heavy changes to the Windows experience to make it both more fluent and stable.

amberOS 11 24H2

Why AstroOS

amberOS is made to give life to old PC's/laptops and boosts the performance of the OS so your computer won't be as slow as it was. It also improves the visual experience to make it more fluent while giving that performance boosts mentioned. If you have a slow computer or want to make the OS look better, download it!

No malware. At all.
